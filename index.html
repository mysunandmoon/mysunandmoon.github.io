The War for Peace

Imagination is the only weapon against reality. The tears of today reflect the sparks of tomorrow. Go forth to your mind palace. Lay waste to worry and put out its fires. At the very corner of reality, a fragment of hope and joy can be found. You must stand not with the perpetual sadness, lest you be immobilized by gravity. Wage war on gravity, remembering who you are. Love is a gift you give to yourself.

Light's Path

You are the source that illuminates the lighthouse. By spirit, you are deathless, imperishable. What a shame it is to shroud your existence, like the Moon under a cloud. Bring out your magnificence. Let the fire within you rekindle once more. Lose yourself. Escape your earthly form and pierce the skies. A speck of your light can reignite the Sun and swallow darkness whole. Enter the silence and do not fear this loss. Rise and embrace the endless heavens.

The Mystery of Love

Though we seek to explain love, when we fall in love, our words fall short, as love unexplained is clearer. Practice the art of love. Keep its mysteries concealed, for only love can truly explain love. Be a rider of true love, and never fear the way. Never underestimate its swift pace, as a single bound can bring us home, though the road may be rocky and uneven.
